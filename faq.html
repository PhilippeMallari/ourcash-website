  • addHow to repay your loan

    RD pawnshop or M.LHUILLIER
    1. Go to RD pawnshop or M.LHUILLIER
    2. Fill up the Bills payment form using the contract/control no. from your app

    7 ELEVEN (7-11)
    1. Find a 7-11 (7 Eleven) or other stores with EC-pay (CLIQQ App)
    2. Click the Bills payment option then select Skypay.
    3. Fill up the payment form with your contract no., mobile no, and payment method
    4. Print the bill then proceed to the cashier to pay the amount indicated
    Note: If the EC-pay machine is offline. you can install the CLIQQ app. Download Here
    1. Open the CLIQQ app.
    2. Select Loans and Skypay.
    3. Fill up the payment form with your contract no., mobile no, and payment method
    4. Click confirm to create a barcode
    5. Present the barcode to the cashier to pay the amount indicated
    GCASH APP
    1. Open the GCASH app.
    2. Select pay Bills. Loans and then Skypay.
    3. Fill up the payment form with your contract no., mobile no, email address and payment amount.
    4. Click confirm to comnplete the transaction. Wait for the SMS confirmation that your payment has been processed.

    WHY WAS MY APPLICATION DENIED? CAN I APPLY AGAIN?

    We are sorry that your application has not been approved. Please be advised that our evaluation is based on the information you provided, the analysis of our evaluators and the risk control system. You can apply 7 days later and make sure that the information provided is accurate and complete. We look forward to serving you again.

      3. Answer: In this situation, please ask the cashier to call our customer service to get help.
